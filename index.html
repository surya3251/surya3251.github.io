What is Chess?

Chess is a strategic board game played between two players on an 8×8 board containing 64 squares. Each player starts with 16 pieces. The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king.

Chess Pieces

♔ King

Moves one square in any direction. Protect the king at all costs.

♕ Queen

Strongest piece. Moves horizontally, vertically, and diagonally.

♖ Rook

Moves horizontally and vertically. Very powerful in endgames.

♗ Bishop

Moves diagonally. Always stays on the same color.

♘ Knight

Moves in an L-shape. Can jump over pieces.

♙ Pawn

Moves one square forward, captures diagonally, can promote upon reaching the last rank.

Basic Rules

  • White moves first.
  • Protect your king.
  • Checkmate wins the game.
  • Stalemate is a draw.
  • Touch-move rule in official games.

Best Piece Combinations

Queen + King

Simplest checkmate. Push enemy king to edge.

Rook + King

Basic endgame every player should know.

Two Rooks

Ladder Mate. Very easy checkmate.

Queen + Rook

Nearly impossible to escape.

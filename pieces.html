♔ The King

Movement

The King moves exactly one square in any direction. Forward, backward, sideways and diagonally.

Special Move

The King can perform Castling with a rook.

Piece Value

Infinite. The King cannot be traded. If checkmated, the game ends.

Tips

  • Castle early.
  • Protect the king.
  • Bring the king into the center during endgames.

♕ The Queen

Movement

Moves horizontally, vertically, and diagonally any number of squares.

Piece Value

9 Points

Advantages

  • Strongest attacking piece.
  • Controls many squares.
  • Excellent for checkmate attacks.

Tips

  • Don't bring the queen out too early.
  • Support with minor pieces.
  • Keep it active.

♖ The Rook

Movement

Moves horizontally and vertically.

Piece Value

5 Points

Strength

  • Very powerful in open files.
  • Strongest endgame piece after queen.
  • Excellent for ladder checkmate.

♗ Bishop

Movement

Moves diagonally. Always remains on one color.

Piece Value

3 Points

Tips

  • Bishop pair is extremely strong.
  • Long diagonal attacks.
  • Works well with queen.

♘ Knight

Movement

Moves in an L shape. Two squares then one square. Can jump over pieces.

Piece Value

3 Points

Special Ability

Only piece that can jump. Excellent for forks.

♙ Pawn

Movement

Moves one square forward. First move can be two squares. Captures diagonally.

Special Rules

  • Promotion
  • En Passant

Piece Value

1 Point

Piece Values

Piece Value

Knight 3
Bishop 3
Rook 5
Queen 9
King ∞

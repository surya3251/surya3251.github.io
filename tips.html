Opening Tips

  1. Control the center with pawns.
  2. Develop knights before bishops.
  3. Castle as early as possible.
  4. Don't move the same piece repeatedly.
  5. Avoid bringing your queen out too early.
  6. Connect your rooks.
  7. Complete development before attacking.
  8. Don't grab risky pawns.
  9. Fight for central squares.
  10. Know your opening ideas, not just moves.

Middlegame Tips

  1. Look for checks first.
  2. Then captures.
  3. Then threats.
  4. Improve your worst piece.
  5. Create a plan.
  6. Watch your opponent's ideas.
  7. Keep your king safe.
  8. Use open files for rooks.
  9. Don't rush attacks.
  10. Coordinate your pieces.

Endgame Tips

  1. Activate your king.
  2. Push passed pawns.
  3. Cut off the enemy king.
  4. Know opposition.
  5. Trade pieces when ahead.
  6. Avoid unnecessary pawn moves.
  7. Learn basic rook endings.
  8. Calculate accurately.
  9. Don't panic in equal positions.
  10. Practice basic mates.

Common Beginner Mistakes

  • Ignoring king safety.
  • Moving the queen too early.
  • Ignoring opponent threats.
  • Leaving pieces undefended.
  • Playing too fast.
  • Not calculating.
  • Ignoring tactics.
  • Not castling.
  • Greedy captures.
  • Giving unnecessary checks.

Tournament Tips

  • Sleep well before games.
  • Stay calm after mistakes.
  • Manage your clock.
  • Record moves carefully.
  • Never resign too early.
  • Drink enough water.
  • Stay focused.
  • Review your games afterward.
